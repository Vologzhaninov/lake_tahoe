Leave work behind and get out on the largest alpine lake in North America

Lake Tahoe is a major tourist attraction in both Nevada and California!

With its stunning blue waters and surrounding snow-capped peaks, Lake Tahoe is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you're into hiking, biking, kayaking, or simply relaxing on the beach, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Boating is a primary activity in Tahoe in the summer. If you're looking for a more relaxing experience, you can simply lounge on one of the many beaches or take a scenic drive around the lake.

Hiking and kayaking

There are also plenty of hiking and biking trails that offer breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding mountains.

In the summer, the lake is perfect for swimming, kayaking, and fishing.
